Timeline

  • Chinese Influence 500s
    Japanese culture is influenced by China. Chinese writing and characters are introduced.
  • Buddhism 530
    The religion of Buddhism comes to Japan.
  • Prince Shotoku 539
    Prince Shotoku comes into power. He promotes Buddhism and brings peace to Japan.
  • Unification 1590
    Japan is unified under the leadership of Toyotomi Hideyoshi. He establishes the Edo Shogunate.
  • End of WWI 1918
    World War I ends. Japan gains a seat on the Council of the League of Nations.
  • Earthquake 2011
    Earthquake and Tsunami cause extensive damage including radiation leaks from a nuclear plant.
